Spearkers

Alexandre Gaigalas

Software engineer at Yahoo!, enthusiast RESTful architectures and Semantic Web

Yahoo

    Alexander Tabor

    Chief Technical Officer of Peixe Urbano, Peixe Urbano was one of the first collective shopping site in Brazil. He is also, a co-founders of group.

    Peixe Urbano

      Bernard de Luna

      Working as interface design and front-end development for over 10 years, having worked on several projects in Brazil, USA, England, Australia. In the last two years, led the front-end of Petrobras in Rio de Janeiro and is currently the Creative Director and UX DSP Melt in Sao Paulo. 5 years ago also began acting at the training for companies, providing web design courses at the College CCAA and performing in various events such as lectures EDTED, IMaster Intercon, FrontinRio, FrontinBH and others.

      Melt DSP

        Leandro Vieira

        One of the great Brazilian references to WordPress. Founding, partner and Chief Technology Officer Apiki WordPress. Enthusiast and evangelist of the WP. She loves sharing her knowledge through courses, lectures and chats.

        Apiki

          Leo Balter

          Leo Balter is Mozilla Representative, FrontEnd Developer at Estante Virtual and a member of the RioJS. Passionate about the Web and open technologies that integrate, is currently engaged in some open source projects such as B2G, and other MDN-related OpenData in Brazil.

          Estante Virtual

            Zeno Rocha

            He is the co-author of some cool open source projects like jQuery Boilerplate and jQuery Boilerplate e do Wormz (Chrome Experiments). He used to work as a software developer at Petrobras (the largest company in Latin America) and now he works at Globoesporte.com (the most accessed sports website in Brazil). With only 21 years-old he is a reference in Brazil when the subject is front-end development.

            Globoesporte.com
